Privacy Policy for Safe Gallery

Safe Gallery is a local photo and video gallery application. Your privacy is important to us.

Safe Gallery does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All photos and videos accessed by the app remain on the user's device and are used only to provide gallery functionality.

The app does not upload media to external servers, does not use analytics, and does not share data with third parties.

Permissions:
- Photos / Media access: required to allow users to browse and manage their own photos and videos stored on the device.
